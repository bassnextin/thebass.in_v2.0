A brief about the company

To solve the problem of security and scalability of data sharing, storing, and synchronizing online, a company from Bhubaneswar, Odisha started developing a clean piece of technology. Plan A Wish is a company which provides its platform as online file storage and synchronization service. PAW allows users to store files in the cloud, synchronize files across devices, and share files. In addition to a web interface, they're are also developing their mobile app for both Android and iOS.
