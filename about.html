Our Story

Started as an idea of 4 restless youth minds, The BASS is a one-stop solution to cater to all youth's temporary but essential and feasible requirements! The BASS as an idea is revolutionary in itself. It’s rather a “SPACE” than a corporation. We believe in Sustainability & How ours, being an Online Platform promotes it! The Space is where all the hungry minds assemble. That hunger is for knowledge, excellence, and bringing about a positive change, however diminutive it may be. Smart Effort is all we seek. And where do all these factors lead? Revolution!
