Project info

Lariox Pharma is a pharmasceutical company which aims to produce and supply its medicure products all across the country. To showcase their brand, they have to create its identity and face. Also, they wanted to design their product lines, so that it will be easier for them to share their aim and story to the customers.

A brief about the company

To survive from different fungal and viral diseases we need proper medicure with appropriate formulations. Keeping this in mind, Laiox Pharma has started its journey to cure the viral diseases and save humankind. Through proper research and formulation this pharma company aims to disrupt the pharma industry on next level. From distribution stores to healthcare checkups, from offline retailers to online stores they are trying to avail medicure to each corner of the country.
