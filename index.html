Who We Are

The BASS, [BASS NextIn (P) Ltd.] is a Bhubaneswar based startup incorporated on 12th Aug, 2020. We are a team of creative and enthusiastic people. We started this startup with one goal in mind to help businesses, new startups, and organizations in digital transformation. We offer consultancy and end-to-end solutions for online setup, from branding to digital exposure with a proven strategy. We aim to meet all the basic and feasible requirements of our customers.

Our services expands from consulting to building a brand which includes branding, digital marketing, graphics designing, software development, technical consulting, and startup support. We are also developing marketplaces to help people in different sectors like hospitality, travel, fashion & lifestyle, electronics, and many more in the digital revolution.

We support Make in India, Digital India, Atmanirbhar Bharat, Vocal for Local, and other campaigns run by Indian Government. We're also recognized and certified by both Startup India and Startup Odisha.
