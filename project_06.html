Project info

Jr. Teji is a subsidiary brand showroom of Tirupati Cloth Store. They aim to offer branded ready made fashion collections to their customers. Our main focus was to design their brand identity and covert their vision into a story which will be shown by the design of the brand itself. We also had responsibility to market their products online through our marketing strategies and showcase the brands to the millenials.

A brief about the company

On a mission to offer millenials trending readymade fashion products, Jr. Teji was started as a subsidiary of Tirupati ClothStore which aims to supply the best fashionable and best quality readymade fashion products across the country. From its first cloth store to its showroom, it's building its brand to its proper reach.Through digital marketing they are supplying ethnic wears with different trending tastes throughout.
